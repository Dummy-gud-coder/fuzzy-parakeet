Quantitative Meme Propagation Models

Meme dissemination follows epidemic modeling principles where transmission rate (β\betaβ) exceeds decay rate (γ\gammaγ).

1. Lifecycle Phase Performance

  1. Seeding Phase: 1–100 initial impressions within 15 minutes.
  2. Amplification Phase: Share-to-view ratio exceeds 0.05 (5%).
  3. Saturation Phase: Engagement decays by 15% per 24-hour cycle after peak reach.

2. Media Format Efficiency

  • Image Macros (PNG/WebP): Mean payload size 250 KB; baseline conversion latency < 100 ms.
  • Short Video Loops (MP4/WebM): Ideal duration 3.0 to 7.0 seconds; 1080x1920 resolution (9:16 aspect ratio); bitrate cap 4.5 Mbps.
