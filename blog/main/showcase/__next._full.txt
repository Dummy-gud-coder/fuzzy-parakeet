## Mathematical Equations ($\LaTeX$)

Mathematical expressions are rendered using KaTeX.

### Inline Math
The classic mass-energy equivalence equation is $E = mc^2$, where $c \approx 3 \times 10^8 \text{ m/s}$.

### Display Math
Standalone formulas render center-aligned with equation formatting:

$$f(x) = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} \hat{f}(\xi) \, e^{2\pi i \xi x} \, d\xi$$

Or matrices and linear systems:

$$\begin{bmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} x \\ y \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} ax + by \\ cx + dy \end{bmatrix}$$

def calculate_fibonacci(n: int) -> list[int]:
    """Generates Fibonacci sequence up to n terms."""
    sequence = [0, 1]
    while len(sequence) < n:
        sequence.append(sequence[-1] + sequence[-2])
    return sequence[:n]
